Why you can trust it with your people

No testimonials yet, because we would rather earn your trust than borrow it. Here is the honest case instead.

Private by architecture

No account, no Sovenn server, no analytics, no trackers. Your contacts live only on your phone, or, if you turn it on, your own Google Drive. No Sovenn server ever sees them.

You are always in control

Sovenn drafts the message. You read it, change it, and tap send yourself, every single time. It never messages anyone for you.

Made by one person, in India

Built around WhatsApp for the way we actually keep in touch here. A small, honest tool, not a data business.

Honest answers

Is this just another app that nags me?
No. Sovenn shows who is quietly drifting, then steps back. No streaks to protect, no red badges of guilt, no "you are 6 days overdue." When you have reached everyone, it simply says you are all caught up.
Where does my data go?
Onto your device, and nowhere else unless you say so. There is no account and no Sovenn server. Your contacts live on your device, with optional backup to your own Google Drive. The only other thing that ever leaves is the message you choose to send, through WhatsApp, by your own tap.
Is it free?
Yes, free to use. Later there may be a small one-time "buy me a chai" thank-you for anyone who wants to support it, but the app and everything that matters stays free.
Does it work on iPhone?
Sovenn runs in any modern mobile browser, so you can open it on iPhone today from the button above. The polished Android app comes first on Google Play, with more to follow.
